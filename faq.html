Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our free online memory games.

Are your free memory games mobile-friendly?

Yes. All our online memory games are fully responsive and designed to work perfectly on desktops, tablets, and smartphones. You can play on any device with a modern web browser.

How are my scores for the memory game saved?

Your best scores (moves and time) are stored directly in your browser using localStorage. This means your progress is saved on your device automatically.

Can I reset my memory game scores?

Yes. To clear your locally stored scores, open your browser's developer console and run the command: localStorage.removeItem('sl_leaderboard'). Then refresh the page.

How do I play the online memory game?

Simply click or tap on the cards to flip them. The game is designed for intuitive mouse and touch controls. No complex keyboard commands are required.

Are the memory games really free to play?

Absolutely. All memory games on our site are completely free to play. We are committed to providing a fun and accessible brain-training experience for everyone without any cost.
